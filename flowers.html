Flowers & Their Powers

Most flowers are edible. They hold therapeutic values. Banana flower used in curries is helpful in treating tuberculosis and reduces excess heat. Cloves used as spice are good for digestive and resporatory diseases. Lavender and jasmine are known to aid in digestive issues. Flowers like Jasmine can also help reduce anxiety, insomnia and stress as do other flowers such as carnation and gardenia. Learn more about flower powers below:
